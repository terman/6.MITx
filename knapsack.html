The Buglar's Dilema

Being a burglar isn't easy! Our friend above has just entered a mansion and is trying to decide what to take. Unfortunately his knapsack only holds 20kg. What strategy should he use in picking objects so as to maximize the value of his haul?

Let's try out three possible strategies. Considering the items remaining to be taken, the burglar chooses to take the item according to

  1. "Max Value" -- choose the item with the greatest value
  2. "Min Weight" -- choose the time with the least weight
  3. "Max Value/Weight" -- choose the item with the largest value-to-weight ratio
